# Retail Store Planogram Platform

This repository documents the architecture and design of an enterprise-grade planogram and store layout platform for supermarkets and large retail operations.

## Scope
- Store layout and planogram modeling
- Product placement rules and constraints
- Operational execution and auditing
- Analytics-driven optimization

## Typical Use Cases
- Supermarket shelf and aisle planning
- Category management
- Store execution validation
- Compliance and operational audits

## Design Principles
- Planograms treated as data, not static drawings
- Rule-driven product placement
- Store-specific variations and constraints
- Full traceability and auditability

## Audience
Retail architects, operations teams, merchandising teams, and technology partners in supermarket and retail chains.

## Repository Structure

- **docs/** - Comprehensive guides on planogram concepts, store modeling, placement rules, operations, and analytics
- **diagrams/** - Visual flows for planogram lifecycle, product placement, store updates, and analytics
- **adr/** - Architecture Decision Records documenting key design choices
- **examples/** - Real-world data models, store layouts, placement rules, compliance checks, and audit reports
- **glossary/** - Retail and planogram terminology
